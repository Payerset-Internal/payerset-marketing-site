The new CMS machine-readable file schema is a significant step forward. Here's what changed, what's better, and the three things every health system needs to do now.

## What's new in Schema 2.0

The updated schema introduces standardized formatting for negotiated rates, clearer provider identification, and better linkage between in-network and out-of-network files.

## What you need to do

1. Audit your current MRF publishing pipeline
2. Update your data extraction tools for the new field structure
3. Validate your files against the new schema before the compliance deadline

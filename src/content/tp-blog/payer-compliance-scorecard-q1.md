Which payers are publishing complete, machine-readable files — and which are still hiding behind PDFs and broken links? We ranked the top 20.

## Methodology

We evaluated the top 20 payers by covered lives across five dimensions: file completeness, schema compliance, update frequency, accessibility, and data quality.

## Top performers

A handful of payers are setting the standard for what good compliance looks like. Their files are complete, regularly updated, and genuinely machine-readable.

## Room for improvement

Too many payers are still publishing files that technically exist but are practically useless — buried behind registration walls, split into thousands of fragments, or formatted in ways that resist automated analysis.
